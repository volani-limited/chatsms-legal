Privacy Policy

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how ChatSMS (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles your information.
We're registered with the UK data protection authority (the Information Commissioner’s Office or ICO) under number ZB581020.

2. Information We Collect

  • Messages you send: We forward your SMS messages to the OpenAI API to generate replies and retain them in your account so you can view your conversation history later. OpenAI stores messages for up to 30 days.
  • Your telephone number: Stored so we can reply to you.
  • Your email address: Stored when you top up your account, to manage billing and resolve issues.
  • Usage data: Aggregate statistics (e.g., number of messages sent, top-ups) to improve the Service.
  • Support inquiries: Retained only as long as necessary to assist you.

3. Why We Collect Data (Legal Bases)

  • Messages & account data: Necessary to perform our contract with you and to provide your chat history. OpenAI does not train its models on your data.
  • Billing records: Necessary for contract and to comply with legal obligations (e.g. tax law).
  • Usage statistics: Our legitimate interest in improving the Service.
  • Support: Necessary for contract and our legitimate interest in resolving issues.
  • Marketing (if you opt in): Based on your consent.

4. Retention of Data

  • Messages: Retained so you can view and manage your conversation history. You may delete individual chats at any time, or delete your account to remove all of your chats from our systems.
  • Billing records: Retained for 6 years in line with UK accounting requirements.
  • Support records: Up to 12 months after resolution.
  • Usage statistics: Retained in aggregate only (non-identifiable).

5. Deleting Your Data

When you delete a chat or close your account, we remove all associated personal data we hold about you from our systems (including your messages, phone number, and account details), except where retention is required by law (e.g., billing records). Deleted items are removed from our active systems promptly and from backups in the normal course of our backup rotation.

6. Who Sees the Data

  • OpenAI: Processes messages via their API. According to OpenAI’s API policy, messages are not used to train their models and are generally retained for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring.
  • Payment Processor: Receives only necessary payment details.
  • Google Firestore: Hosts our database, with encryption in transit and at rest.
  • Internal team: Access limited to support and maintenance purposes.

7. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred outside the UK/EU (e.g., to OpenAI in the US or Firestore data centres). Where this occurs, we rely on UK adequacy regulations or UK-approved Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) to protect your data.

8. Data Security
